hi, i'm angela!

i'm a third year computer science major with a design minor at the university of california, san diego. my interest in web design began when i was just 13 years old, constantly tweaking the html and css of tumblr themes. my goal is to combine my passion for design and computer science by pursuing front-end development.

i'm a big advocate for academic retention and building community. i am a programming assistant on the academic pillar of ucsd's filipino cultural org, kaibigang pilipin@. i also worked as a residential assistant from fall 2019 to winter 2020; i am hoping to return to my job as an residential assistant in the fall, in spite of the recent pandemic.

i also love the color yellow, the sims, typing in lowercase letters, and treating people with kindness.
