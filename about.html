hi there!
i'm angela (she/her/hers).

i'm a third year student studying computer science and minoring in design at uc san diego.

my passion for web development began when my 13 year old self discovered how to edit html on tumblr. eventually, i grew out of using tumblr, but i continued coding -- taking ap computer science in high school and ultimately deciding that i should try majoring in it.

fast forward to fall of 2019. i was really unsure about my future in computer science; i took a web language elective while struggling with the idea of changing my major. taking this class and learning the fundamentals of html and css rekindled my love for web development. it showed me that my passion was right in the intersection of developing and designing. this led me to also declare a minor in design. now here i am, actively working towards improving my skills as a front end developer. :)

when i'm not meticulously editing html and css, i'm advocating for academic retention and building community. i am a programming assistant on the academic pillar of ucsd's filipino cultural org, kaibigang pilipin@. i also worked as a residential assistant from fall 2019 to winter 2020; i am hoping to return to my job as an residential assistant in the fall, in spite of the recent pandemic.
